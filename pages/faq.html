Frequently Asked Questions:

Can I post my own properties?

Yes! you can always add how many properties you have for rent. Check this section for more details

Can I change my review after I send submit?

Once you hit "send" our reviewers will have 24 hours to approve it or reject it. Once that timeframe has passed, you'll get a notification via email, which you can click to edit the review and start the process one more time.

Is it possible that my review remains anonymous?

Of course! When filling the review form, just mark on the "I want this review to be anonymous" section.
